Walking

Walking can improve your health and wellbeing, and can help you to live independently for longer.

Being able to walk without help is one of the strongest indicators of whether someone can live independently.

Yoga

Yoga refreshes your mind and spirit. Tones your body. Keeps your internal organs and hormonal system in balance. All the more reason for people of all ages to do yoga.

Food

Ageing decreases calorie needs. However, the nutrient demands remain the same and, in some cases, increase. Subsequently, you must eat nutrient-rich food which will help you get vitamins, minerals, carbohydrates, fats, and protein.




Meditation

For centuries now, meditation has been used to help people feel more at peace. And in recent years, meditation has increased in popularity due to its scientifically-proven benefits. In fact, according to the National Institutes of Health, the number of adults practicing meditation more than tripled in five years, from 4.1% to 14.2%.

The most common types of meditation include:

  • Loving-kindness meditation.
  • Mantra meditation.
  • Mindfulness meditation.
  • Movement meditation (Yoga).
  • Spiritual meditation.
  • Transcendental meditation.

I'll give some practical tips for you to keep in mind, when it comes to blood tests.

  1. Complete Blood Count(CBC):

    What it measures: A CBC is a collection of tests related to the cells in your blood.

  2. Basic Metabolic Panel:

    Although it's possible to request a measurement of a single electrolyte, it's far more common for electrolytes to be ordered as part of a panel of seven or eight measurements. This is often referred to as a “chem-7,”

  3. Comprehensive Metabolic Panel:

    This panel includes the items above in the basic metabolic panel, and then usually includes an additional seven items.

  4. Lipid Panel:

    These tests measure the different types of cholesterol and related fats in the bloodstream.
